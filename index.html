Saving for Retirement and Living in Retirement Are Two Different Challenges

The Opportunity
The pre-retirement window — roughly ages 50 to 65 — is when proactive planning has the most flexibility to make a difference. Roth conversion strategy, RMD modeling, and IRMAA analysis done now can help you make more informed decisions across your lifetime.

We design a retirement income strategy aimed at sequencing your withdrawals thoughtfully, addressing tax exposure across your lifetime, and built around your actual spending needs and individual goals — not just a projected account balance at age 90.

  • ✓
    Retirement Income Strategy: A structured approach to drawing from your accounts as income, with tax efficiency as a key consideration. Built around how you actually intend to live and your individual goals.
  • ✓
    IRMAA-Adjusted Roth Analysis: We include Medicare surcharge impact in every conversion analysis, which is often omitted from standard planning software defaults.
  • ✓
    Proactive RMD Strategy: We model Required Minimum Distribution trajectories early, so you aren't reacting to forced taxable income at 73.
  • ✓
    Cash Flow Modeling: Real math, not optimistic charts. We match your income sources, spending, taxes, and healthcare costs over time.
